How can you tell if your air ducts need to be cleaned?

If you’re like most homeowners, you probably don’t think about your air ducts very often. But if you’re not sure whether or not they need to be cleaned, now is a good time to take a look. In this blog post, we’ll discuss some of the signs that your air ducts may need to be cleaned. We’ll also provide some tips on how to get them cleaned if necessary. So if you’re wondering whether or not it’s time for a duct cleaning, keep reading!

The telltale signs that your air ducts need to be cleaned.

If you’ve smoked in your home, even for a short time, your air ducts probably need to be cleaned. This is because smoke contains toxins that can cling to the air ducts and lessen their performance. Cigarette smoke isn’t the only type of pollution that your air ducts might have to deal with. In fact, other contaminants like mold, mildew and dust mites can be even more harmful to your home’s air quality.

Dirty air ducts can affect the performance of your heating and cooling system. For example, they might make it harder for your heater or AC filters to do their job because they restrict airflow. Over time, dirty air ducts can lead to higher energy bills and more frequent maintenance costs. If you’re worried that your air ducts are dirty, it’s a good idea to have them cleaned right away. Doing so can help improve the performance of your heating and cooling system which means lower energy bills and less stress on your HVAC unit.


How often should your air ducts be cleaned, on average?

As a general rule of thumb, it’s best to have your air ducts cleaned every two years. This is important because you spend about 90% of your time indoors so the quality of the air in your home really matters. If you have allergies or other respiratory conditions, however, perhaps consider having them cleaned more often. This is especially true if your symptoms tend to get worse when the seasons change. If you have several people living in your home, consider having them cleaned every year. This is because there will be more contaminants floating through the air which means a greater risk of exposure to allergens and other pollutants.

If you’re not sure how often your air ducts need to be cleaned, it’s advisable to get them inspected and professionally cleaned at least once every two years.

What are the benefits of having clean Air Ducts?

The benefits of having your air ducts cleaned are numerous. First, it’s an effective way to boost the quality of the air in your home. Since pollutants can cause respiratory problems and other health concerns, this is definitely something you want to avoid! This is particularly true if you have anyone in your home who might be more susceptible to allergies or respiratory conditions, including young children.

Having your air ducts professionally cleaned can also help improve the performance of your heating and cooling system. This means lower energy bills and less stress on your HVAC unit, which is definitely something to keep in mind. Finally, it’s always nice to be able to breathe clean air so if you suffer from allergies or other respiratory problems, you might consider having your air ducts cleaned by a professional.

What are some things to keep in mind when choosing a company to clean your air ducts?

It’s generally a good idea to hire a company that is certified by NADCA or the EPA. This certification will ensure that your air ducts are cleaned in an effective and professional way. Ask for referrals from friends, family members, co-workers or neighbors who have recently had their air ducts professionally cleaned. They should be able to provide you with the name of a company that did an excellent job for them.

When you’re looking into companies to clean your air ducts, inquire about their prices and whether or not they provide discounts. Experience is another thing to keep in mind because it’s important to hire someone who has cleaned air ducts before and knows how to get the job done. It’s also a wise idea to find out what sort of equipment they’ll be using and whether or not they’re going to need to move any of your furniture.

Before you hire a company to clean your air ducts, make sure that it is insured in case damage occurs. If necessary, hire a team who will provide you with a written estimate that details the cost of cleaning your air ducts.
